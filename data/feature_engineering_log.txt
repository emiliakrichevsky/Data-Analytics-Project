FEATURE ENGINEERING LOG
======================================================================

1. daily_return: Stock daily return (pct_change)
2. r_1W: 1-week (5-day) return
3. r_1M: 1-month (21-day) return
4. r_3M: 3-month (63-day) return
5. vol_1M: 1-month volatility (rolling std of daily returns)
6. MA20_ratio: Close / MA20
7. MA50_ratio: Close / MA50
8. HL_range: (High - Low) / Close
9. vol_z: Volume z-score (Volume - mean(20)) / std(20)
10. sp500_daily_return: SP500 daily return
11. sp500_vol_1M: SP500 1-month volatility
12. relative_return: stock_return - sp500_return
13. volatility_ratio: stock_vol / sp500_vol
14. CPI_chg: CPI percentage change
15. FedFunds_chg: Federal Funds Rate change (absolute)
16. VIX_t: VIX index (direct use)
17. FedFunds_t: Federal Funds Rate (direct use)
18. Put_Call_Ratio_t: Put/Call Ratio (direct use)
19. Market_Breadth_t: Market Breadth (direct use)
20. spy_RSI_t: SPY RSI (direct use)
21. spy_SMA_50_t, spy_SMA_200_t: SPY moving averages
22. qqq_RSI_t: QQQ RSI (direct use)
23. qqq_SMA_50_t, qqq_SMA_200_t: QQQ moving averages
24. sector_XLK_t, sector_XLF_t, etc.: Sector ETF prices (direct use)
